Mesmerizing Mulpokhri - A Camping Experience to cherish

by Pratik Sengupta

Posted on August 28, 2013 at 10:45 PM

Mul Pokhari, other than being an amazing campsite and a birderâ€™s paradise, also is a gateway to Neora Valley National Park, the last of the few remaining bio-diversities in our country and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. This pristine forest, still largely unexplored, offers protection to more than 31 species of mammals, 250 species of birds, 276 species of insects, and, 38 species of other invertebrates.
